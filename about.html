About Ghosxt

Ghosxt brings enterprise-level IT infrastructure and security expertise to small and medium-sized businesses. We started because we kept seeing the same problem: businesses either get commodity support that falls apart under pressure, or they can't access the level of expertise they actually need.

We bridge that gap. Ghosxt is built around a single DoD-cleared engineer with experience managing IT for everything from large-scale organizations to small local businesses, applying that same rigor to every client—without the enterprise consulting fees.

Our Services

Comprehensive IT Management

Whether it's detecting threats in real-time, preventing breaches before they happen, or automating your security operations, Ghosxt makes every interaction safer and every team more secure.

Enterprise Experience

Our approach is shaped by years of working with large-scale IT infrastructure:

  • Enterprise OS deployments and migrations across secure environments
  • Advanced Active Directory architecture and Group Policy management
  • PowerShell automation for complex system deployments
  • Network infrastructure for multi-site organizations
  • Security compliance frameworks and implementation
  • Managing diverse client needs across multiple industries

This experience directly benefits Ghosxt clients. The same practices that work for large organizations—proper documentation, security-first design, thorough testing, systematic problem-solving—work just as well for small businesses. They just need to be adapted appropriately.

Small Business Focus

We've also managed IT for 40+ small businesses, which taught us how to:

  • Design infrastructure that scales efficiently without over-engineering
  • Balance cost constraints with proper IT architecture
  • Solve problems across every major business system and vendor
  • Communicate clearly without technical jargon
  • Provide strategic guidance, not just technical support

This combination—enterprise expertise plus small business experience—means we understand both what good IT looks like AND how to deliver it cost-effectively.

How We Work

What Makes Our Approach Different

1

Direct Communication

We explain what's happening with your systems in plain English. If something breaks, we tell you what broke, why it broke, and what we're doing to fix it permanently—not just patch it temporarily. No runaround, no jargon, no excuses.

2

Enterprise Architecture for Small Business

The infrastructure practices that work for large organizations—proper Active Directory design, network segmentation, security hardening, systematic monitoring—work just as well for small businesses. You get enterprise-quality infrastructure without paying for a 20-person IT department.

3

Proactive, Not Reactive

Custom monitoring systems catch issues before they impact your business. Rather than waiting for systems to break, we identify patterns and fix problems during maintenance windows—not during your busiest workday.

4

Complete Documentation

We document everything about your infrastructure. Network topology, configurations, passwords, procedures—all organized and accessible. You're never locked in or left guessing if something happens to us.

5

Transparent Pricing

You always know what you're paying and why. No surprise bills, no nickel-and-diming for every small request, no hidden fees. Managed services are predictable monthly costs, and project work is quoted upfront.

Our Name

Why "Ghosxt"

The Concept

We wanted a name that reflected the behind-the-scenes nature of good IT infrastructure. When IT is working properly, you shouldn't have to think about it—it just works. The ghost represents that: quietly effective, always present, protecting your business, but never intrusive. Plus, it's memorable. And being memorable matters when you're building long-term relationships with clients.

Behind Ghosxt

About Ulises

Ulises Paiz
Founder & CEO

Ulises Paiz built Ghosxt on a simple idea: the businesses in his community deserve the same level of protection that the Department of Defense demands. With over 10 years in IT infrastructure and cybersecurity, a B.S. in Network Engineering and Cybersecurity, an Active Top Secret Clearance, and nine industry certifications including CySA+, Security+, and AZ-104, he brings federal-grade discipline to every environment he touches.

Before founding Ghosxt, Ulises served as a Senior Solutions Consultant for the DoD, managed infrastructure for a multinational corporation across North America, and built security programs for over 40 businesses. He also coaches football at Rancho San Juan High School because the same principles that protect networks protect teams: preparation, trust, and accountability.
