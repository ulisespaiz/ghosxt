Villicana Group — Transportation & Logistics Company

Villicana Group is a Central Coast transportation company, and one of the businesses Ghosxt has partnered with longest — a relationship spanning three years across nearly every part of their IT. The work has run from website and cybersecurity to meeting high-level compliance standards and improving the efficiency of their security systems, with the import/export side of a logistics business adding its own demands.

It is the kind of ongoing partnership we aim for: not a vendor who appears when something breaks, but a single engineer who understands the whole business and helps it adapt to the technology side over time. For a transportation and logistics operation, that continuity is the value — the systems that move freight and clear customs cannot be handed to a stranger each time.

“We've had the pleasure of partnering with Ghosxt for the past three years, and it's been an incredibly valuable relationship. Ulises has provided us with consistent, personalized IT support that's helped us better understand and adapt to the tech side of our business. From customizing our website to enhancing our cybersecurity, he's played a key role in helping us meet high-level compliance standards. He's also helped streamline our import/export logistics and improve the overall efficiency of our security systems.”

— Villicana Group, Transportation & Logistics Company

C&M Machine Shop Inc. — Machine Shop, California

Machine shops run on systems that have to stay up and data that is expensive to lose — job files, designs, and the day-to-day tools the floor depends on. C&M Machine Shop turned to Ghosxt for IT support that treats them as a partner rather than a ticket number, with someone who takes the time to explain what is happening and find the right solution rather than the quickest one.

This is the relationship we want every manufacturing and machine-shop client to have: on-demand support that is fast and reliable, and an engineer invested in the business for the long run.

“Ulises is not just a service provider — he's a true partner who takes the time to explain, support, and find the right solutions for our needs. We're grateful for the continued trust and collaboration with Ghosxt and look forward to what's ahead.”

— C&M Machine Shop Inc., Machine Shop, California

Andrew Sandoval — Salinas City Council Member

Not every project is a multi-year managed-IT engagement. For Salinas City Council Member Andrew Sandoval, the work was a website — and the experience reflects how we approach any project, large or small: take the time to understand the vision, deliver it responsively and professionally, and add the thoughtful touches that make the result better than expected.

Whether the job is a single website or a complete IT and security program, the standard is the same.

“I had an incredible experience working with Ulises! He was responsive, professional, and truly took the time to understand my vision. Not only did he bring my ideas to life, but he also added thoughtful touches that made the site even better than I imagined.”

— Andrew Sandoval, Salinas City Council Member
