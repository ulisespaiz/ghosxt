Microsoft 365 and cloud services we handle for Carmel

Carmel businesses run lean — a gallery owner managing inventory from an iPad, a real estate agent on the road, inn staff accessing schedules from the floor. Microsoft 365 is the right platform for all of that, but it needs to be built correctly: hardened against the specific risks these businesses carry, and set up so it actually gets used rather than worked around.

Microsoft 365 Setup & Hardening

A fresh Microsoft 365 tenant configured from the ground up with the security baselines Microsoft ships disabled by default — phishing-resistant MFA, Conditional Access policies, legacy authentication blocked, and a Secure Score that reflects the actual risk posture of a Carmel small business rather than the default out-of-box state.

Email & File Migration

Moving from on-prem Exchange, Google Workspace, or a shared hosting inbox to Microsoft 365 without disrupting a busy inn or gallery during season. Mailboxes, shared mailboxes, calendars, and files are pre-staged and validated before any cutover. The transition is scheduled for the quietest window available, and the team arrives Monday with everything working.

SharePoint, Teams & OneDrive

File storage rebuilt as a SharePoint and Teams structure that matches how a small Carmel team actually works — with permissions set properly, not left open because nobody got around to it. Galleries, inns, and professional offices each get a layout that makes sense for their workflows. See our guide to which to use when.

Secure Document Sharing

SharePoint and OneDrive configured so a Carmel real estate or law office can share, review, and sign confidential documents with clients securely — without those documents circulating in email threads or sitting in a shared inbox with no access controls. Sensitive files stay where they belong, with a clear audit trail.

Gallery & Inn Systems

Inventory management, CRM, booking platforms, and point-of-sale tied into a hardened Microsoft 365 tenant so that gallery staff and front-desk teams work from a single coherent environment. Guest-facing systems are segmented from back-office accounts so that a compromised reservation system does not become a path into the rest of the business.

Azure & Hybrid

Azure deployed where it genuinely earns its place — a line-of-business application, secure remote access for a Carmel Valley office with limited connectivity, or hybrid identity for a firm with both cloud and on-prem systems. We do not add Azure complexity for its own sake; we add it when it solves a specific problem.

Cloud Backup & Identity

Immutable backup for Microsoft 365 data — email, SharePoint, and OneDrive — that Microsoft does not protect the way most businesses assume. Plus Entra ID and Conditional Access configured so that every account is covered, not just the ones someone thought to enroll.

Galleries and boutiques want inventory, CRM, and point-of-sale that sync cleanly across a small team. How we handle the cutover without the Monday chaos

The most common reason a Carmel business stays on an aging email platform is that nobody wants to own the migration risk. A gallery that loses its client contact history or a real estate office whose email goes dark for a day has a real problem. We remove that risk by pre-staging the migration: mailboxes and files sync to the new tenant while the old system keeps running, and we only flip the cutover once everything is confirmed working. The timeline is built around your schedule, not a standard template — if your quiet window is a Tuesday in January when the tasting rooms are slow, that is when we do it.

Permissions, shared mailboxes, calendar delegation, and any line-of-business integrations are tested before your team logs in, not discovered broken afterward. And because the same engineer manages the environment after the move, there is no handoff to a stranger when something needs adjusting. The cloud work is part of managed IT, backed by a real help desk.

Cloud managed locally, not handed off to a ticket queue

Microsoft 365 does not manage itself, and a misconfigured tenant that nobody is watching will accumulate problems — stale accounts, lapsed MFA enrollment, backup jobs that stopped running. Our cloud work for Carmel is managed continuously as part of a complete Carmel IT services program, alongside cybersecurity and a real help desk that picks up the phone. When something changes in your environment — a new hire, a departing staff member, a licensing question — it is handled by the same person who built the system.

FAQs about cloud and Microsoft 365 in Carmel

Do you provide Microsoft 365 support for Carmel businesses?
Yes. We set up, harden, and manage Microsoft 365 for Carmel small businesses — email, Teams, SharePoint, OneDrive, and licensing — with the security baselines most setups skip. On-site in Carmel and Carmel Valley, remote nationwide.
Can you migrate our email and files without downtime?
Yes. We migrate email and shared files to Microsoft 365 with the cutover planned around your schedule, usually over a weekend, so your team does not lose a workday. Mailboxes, permissions, and shared files are validated before and after.
Can you set up secure document sharing for a real estate or law office?
