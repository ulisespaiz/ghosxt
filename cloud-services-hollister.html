Microsoft 365 and cloud services set up right for Hollister

Most Hollister businesses acquired Microsoft 365 through a reseller or a Microsoft prompt, clicked through setup, and moved on. Licenses are active, but MFA is still off, the Secure Score is in the basement, and nobody knows what is actually backed up. We start from that reality and fix it — hardened tenant, proper backup, and a structure that fits how a Hollister shop or office actually works, not how the default wizard imagines it.

Microsoft 365 Setup & Hardening

A properly hardened Microsoft 365 tenant looks different from the one a Hollister business typically has in place — phishing-resistant MFA enforced, Conditional Access policies tuned to how staff actually log in, and a Secure Score that reflects real controls rather than Microsoft's default suggestions. We do the full configuration, not just licensing.

Email & File Migration

Hollister businesses moving off a local Exchange server, a web host's shared mail, or an old Google Workspace account need their history intact and their team functional on Monday. We pre-stage mailboxes, calendars, and shared files, validate before the cutover, and confirm afterward — so the move is a non-event for your staff.

SharePoint, Teams & OneDrive

The mapped drive pointing at a server in the back office gets rebuilt as a SharePoint and Teams structure with sensible permissions — field crew sees what they need, office sees what they need, and former employees do not still have access. See our guide to which to use when.

Off the On-Prem Server

Hollister has no shortage of servers running in utility rooms and storage closets — hardware that last saw a firmware update years ago and sits on a UPS nobody has tested. We move file shares, line-of-business data, and shared drives to Microsoft 365 and Azure so a dead drive or a flooded closet does not become a week-long recovery. See agriculture and manufacturing IT for industry context.

Azure & Hybrid

Not every Hollister business can move everything to the cloud at once. For operations that need a cloud host for a line-of-business app, secure remote access for staff commuting to Gilroy or working a remote ranch site, or a hybrid identity setup while an aging server runs out its life — Azure earns its place. We do not push it where it does not fit.

Cloud Backup & Identity

Microsoft 365 does not protect your data the way most owners assume — deleted mailbox items and SharePoint files have short retention windows, and ransomware can reach synced OneDrive files. We add immutable backup for Exchange, SharePoint, and OneDrive, and deploy Entra ID with Conditional Access to close the identity gaps baked into every default Hollister tenant.
