Cloud and Microsoft 365 work we do in Seaside

Most Seaside businesses run Microsoft 365 at the defaults — licensed but not secured, and not set up to actually replace the old PC or shared drive. Some are still running on a single aging PC or a shared Google account that everyone uses. We fix both problems: a clean, hardened Microsoft 365 setup and the migration to get you there without downtime.

Microsoft 365 Setup & Hardening

Email, Teams, SharePoint, and OneDrive configured for a real Seaside business — not just turned on and billed monthly, but hardened with phishing-resistant MFA, Conditional Access, and a Secure Score that reflects actual protection rather than checkbox minimums.

Email & File Migration

Move from a shared hosting account, old Exchange server, or tangled Google Workspace to Microsoft 365 with mailboxes, calendars, and shared files validated start to finish — staged on the weekend so your staff shows up Monday and everything just works.

SharePoint, Teams & OneDrive

The shared drive rebuilt as a sensible SharePoint and Teams structure — named folders, right permissions, no more emailing attachments back and forth or digging through someone's personal OneDrive. See which to use when.

Reliable Back Office for a Busy Seaside Team

Restaurants, retail shops, healthcare offices, and nonprofits with staff who use three different devices in a day need email and documents that work from anywhere, not an office server they can only reach in person. Microsoft 365 handles that — when the licensing is right-sized and the tenant is not left at defaults.

Off Aging Hardware

A lot of Seaside small businesses are running on a PC that is well past its service life, with critical files only on that machine. We move those files into Microsoft 365 with backup built in before the hardware fails — so the next crash is an inconvenience, not a crisis.

Azure & Hybrid

Azure where it solves a real problem — a line-of-business app that needs a hosted environment, secure remote access for a team working across multiple Seaside locations, or hybrid identity management — and not pushed where simpler is the right answer.

Cloud Backup & Identity

Microsoft 365 does not retain deleted data the way most businesses assume. We add immutable backup for Exchange, SharePoint, and Teams data, alongside Entra ID hardening and Conditional Access policies so a stolen password does not become a full account takeover.

Microsoft 365 for the Seaside economy

Retail and restaurants want back-office files that are accessible and reliable, not tied to a single PC in a back room. Auto and repair shops want job records and customer history reachable from the shop floor or a tablet. Healthcare and dental offices need documents available anywhere staff are working, with appropriate access controls. Small offices and nonprofits — often with one person wearing five hats — need email that functions reliably without requiring in-house IT to keep it running. Microsoft 365 delivers a solid productivity and communication layer for all of these when it is configured correctly rather than left at the out-of-box defaults.

What Seaside businesses have in common is that they are busy, run on thin margins, and have no spare time to maintain a server or audit cloud settings. Moving to Microsoft 365 eliminates the server maintenance problem and puts the whole team on a platform with strong security controls — but only when the tenant is configured, not just licensed. That configuration gap is exactly what we close, and it is the part most quick-setup vendors skip.

Getting to the cloud without a Monday-morning disaster

Seaside businesses put off moving to Microsoft 365 because the last thing a restaurant or retail shop can afford is email down on a Monday. We plan around that: mailboxes and files pre-staged, cutover on your schedule — usually a Saturday night — and everything validated before your staff arrives. Shared mailboxes, calendar permissions, and any line-of-business integrations are tested, not assumed. If something is off, it gets caught before the workday, not during it.

Because the same engineer manages the environment after the migration, there is no handoff to a stranger and no knowledge gap. The cloud work sits inside managed IT and a real help desk — so when you have a question in week three, the person who moved you is the one who picks up.

Cloud work that stays managed — no handoff, no drift

A Microsoft 365 tenant that nobody owns after setup drifts fast — security settings change, licenses pile up unused, and shared drives become a mess that nobody will untangle. Our Seaside cloud work is part of the same ongoing program as cybersecurity and a real help desk. The same person who configured it is the one managing it in month six.
