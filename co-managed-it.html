Co-Managed IT Services

You already have IT — maybe one stretched-thin person, maybe a small team — and they are good. They are also the only one who knows how everything works, cannot take a real vacation, and do not have time to become a cybersecurity specialist on top of everything else. Co-managed IT fixes that. Ghosxt works alongside your team, covering the gaps, the after-hours load, and the security and compliance depth, so your internal IT gets stronger, not replaced.

What co-managed IT actually means

Co-managed IT is a partnership, not a takeover. Your internal IT person or team stays in place and stays in charge of what they do best — the institutional knowledge, the relationships, the line-of-business apps they know inside out. We slot in around them to handle the things that quietly burn them out: the overflow tickets, the 2 a.m. alerts, the security stack they never have time to build, and the big projects that need a second pair of expert hands.

The result is a business that no longer depends on one person never getting sick, never quitting, and never going on vacation — while that person finally gets the backup, the tooling, and the specialist support to do their job well.

How we support your IT team

Pick the gaps that hurt most. Co-managed IT is modular — we take on exactly the role your team needs, and nothing they would rather keep.

24/7 Help Desk Overflow

When your IT person is buried, tickets still get answered. Our US-based help desk absorbs the day-to-day volume — password resets, new-hire setups, the everyday questions — so your internal team can focus on the work only they can do.

After-Hours & On-Call Coverage

Nights, weekends, holidays, and vacations covered by a real engineer. Your single IT person stops being a single point of failure, and gets to actually unplug without the business going dark.

Security & Compliance Specialist

A DoD-cleared engineer adds the EDR, identity hardening, immutable backup, and HIPAA, CMMC, PCI, and cyber-insurance documentation that a busy generalist team rarely has the time or specialization to build. See cybersecurity.

Enterprise Tooling & RMM

Bring your team the same monitoring, patching, and remote-management stack we run for fully managed clients — without you buying, integrating, and maintaining it. Your IT person works with better tools overnight.

Project & Migration Surge Capacity

A Microsoft 365 migration, an office move, a server refresh, a backup and DR rebuild — we supply the extra hands and expertise for the big push, then hand the keys back to your team.

Documentation & vCIO Strategy

We document the environment properly and bring vCIO-level planning — budgets, roadmaps, risk — so your internal team is set up to succeed instead of firefighting blind.

Signs co-managed IT is the right fit

If your IT runs on one person who cannot take a vacation without anxiety, you have a single point of failure that one resignation or illness turns into a crisis. If your team is technical but stretched too thin to keep up with patching, security, and projects at once, things are slipping that you cannot see yet. If a cyber-insurer or a big customer is now demanding controls and documentation your team has not had time to build, the clock is already running.

If any of that sounds familiar, co-managed IT gives you enterprise-grade depth and 24/7 coverage without firing anyone or rebuilding from scratch — you keep your people and add a cleared engineering team behind them.

Part of a complete IT program

Co-managed IT draws on the same capabilities we run for fully managed clients — help desk, cybersecurity, Microsoft 365 and cloud, backup and disaster recovery, and vCIO strategy — applied to whichever pieces your team wants to share. As your needs change, the split can change with them.

Co-managed IT FAQs

What's the difference between co-managed and fully managed IT?
With fully managed IT, we run everything. With co-managed IT, you keep your internal IT person or team and we work alongside them — covering the gaps they cannot, taking the after-hours and overflow load, and bringing specialist depth in security and compliance. You stay in control; your team gets backup and breathing room.
Will you replace our internal IT person?
No. Co-managed IT exists to make your internal IT stronger, not to replace anyone. Most of our co-managed clients keep their IT person exactly where they are and use us to remove the burnout, single-point-of-failure risk, and specialist gaps. We make your in-house person look good, not redundant.
Can you just cover nights, weekends, and vacations?
Yes. On-call and after-hours coverage is one of the most common reasons businesses bring us in. Your IT person finally gets to take a real vacation or sleep through the night, knowing a cleared engineer and a 24/7 help desk have the wheel.
Can you bring cybersecurity and compliance expertise our team doesn't have?
Yes — this is where co-managed IT pays for itself. As a DoD-cleared engineer, we add the EDR, identity hardening, immutable backup, and compliance documentation (HIPAA, CMMC, PCI, cyber-insurance) that a generalist internal team rarely has time or specialization to build.
How does co-managed IT pricing work?
It is scoped to exactly the role we play — help-desk overflow, after-hours coverage, security, tooling, or project surge — rather than a blanket per-user fee for everything. Pricing is transparent and agreed upfront. Start with a free assessment and we will propose a clear split of responsibilities and cost.
