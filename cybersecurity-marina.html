The Marina threat picture

Fast-growing companies are breached in predictable ways: identity and access sprawl as headcount climbs, devices added faster than they are secured, and the assumption that there is time to lock things down later. Startups and ag-tech firms hold IP and customer data that draw targeted phishing. Hybrid and student-adjacent teams around CSUMB widen the attack surface with personal devices and shared logins.

The controls that stop this are the same proven set used on government endpoints — phishing-resistant MFA, EDR, hardened identity, immutable backup. The trick for a growing company is building them in early so they scale, instead of bolting them on after an incident forces the issue.

Government-grade controls sized for Marina's growing businesses

Marina's growth corridors — Imjin, the Dunes, Reservation Road — are filling with companies at exactly the stage where security debt accumulates fastest: headcount is climbing, devices are joining the network faster than policies can cover them, and the founding team is still doing everything. The controls that matter are known; the challenge is deploying them before the first incident forces the issue. We build the same hardened baseline used on government endpoints, calibrated for a team of five to fifty.

Endpoint Detection & Response

Huntress EDR backed by a 24/7 SOC, layered with Microsoft Defender, on every endpoint. Marina startups typically have a mix of company devices, personal laptops, and contractor machines — this is the layer that catches ransomware attempting to spread before it reaches the rest of the network.

Identity & MFA

Phishing-resistant MFA, Conditional Access, and least-privilege access locked in while the team is still small — because cleaning up identity sprawl at twenty employees costs far more than getting it right at five. The single highest-return control for a growing Marina company. See identity hardening.

New-Office Buildout Security

New office space in Marina moves fast — leases signed, furniture delivered, and everyone showing up Monday. We set the network segmentation, device baseline, and access policies before the first employee logs in, so the security foundation is in the walls from day one instead of added after the first problem.

Startup & Ag-Tech Readiness

An enterprise buyer or institutional investor sending a security questionnaire is a good problem to have — unless the controls are not in place. We document EDR coverage, MFA enrollment, backup policy, and access controls so a Marina ag-tech or SaaS company can answer the review confidently and close the deal.

Immutable Backup

Air-gapped backups the production environment cannot reach or overwrite, tested on a schedule with verified restore times. For a Marina business without a dedicated IT team, this is what turns a ransomware event into a recoverable incident instead of a shutdown. See backup & DR.

24/7 Monitoring & Response

Alerts without a human behind them are just noise. When something fires at 2 a.m. on a device used by a hybrid Marina team spread across the Dunes office and a home network, it is investigated and contained — not sitting in a queue until morning. The pace of modern attacks does not wait for business hours.
