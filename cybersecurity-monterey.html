The Monterey threat picture

Monterey's economy gives attackers several distinct targets. Hospitality businesses process card data and run seasonal staff, a combination that invites point-of-sale compromise and social engineering. Defense contractors and subcontractors near the Naval Postgraduate School and the Defense Language Institute hold controlled information that raises the stakes well above the average small business. Clinics and dental practices carry protected health information under HIPAA. Professional-services firms hold confidential client material that is damaging if leaked.

The defenses are the same proven controls in every case — what changes is the compliance overlay and the consequences of getting it wrong. That is the part a generic IT shop tends to miss, and the part a cleared engineer is built for.
