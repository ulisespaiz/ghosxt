The Soledad threat picture — wine country and produce land are both targets

Food and produce operations now run connected technology end to end — coolers, processing and packing lines, irrigation, and cold-chain logistics — much of it never designed to be secured, and all of it costly to lose during a harvest window. Vineyards and tasting rooms run point-of-sale, wine-club membership, and e-commerce systems that hold customer card data and personal records. Growers run lean offices that are easy to phish for payment and invoice fraud. The Correctional Training Facility is a large area employer with a substantial contractor and service-business ecosystem — professional services and trades around Soledad face the same credential-theft and business email compromise threats as anywhere.

The defenses are the same proven controls deployed on government endpoints — EDR, hardened identity, immutable backup, and segmentation of operational technology from the office network. What South County has lacked is a provider who will actually show up. Being based in Salinas is exactly what makes that possible.

Cybersecurity built for Soledad's wine and produce economy

Vineyards and tasting rooms hold customer card data, wine-club membership records, and e-commerce credentials that are worth targeting. Produce growers and food processors run connected cold-chain and packing-line technology that can be disrupted at the worst possible moment — harvest. Small offices across South County are phished for payment and invoice fraud because the defenses are thin and the payoff is predictable. The controls that close those gaps are the same ones deployed on government endpoints: EDR, hardened identity, immutable backup, and OT segmentation — sized for a Soledad business, not a federal agency.

Endpoint Detection & Response

Huntress EDR backed by a 24/7 SOC on every endpoint, layered with Microsoft Defender. Ransomware targeting a winery or produce operation is isolated before it reaches the files that matter — customer records, wine-club data, compliance documents.

Identity & MFA

Phishing-resistant MFA and Conditional Access harden every account — the tasting-room manager, the field supervisor, the office bookkeeper. Legacy auth is killed off. This is the single highest-leverage control we deploy. See identity hardening.

Produce & Cold-Chain OT

Coolers, packing lines, and irrigation controls segmented from the office network and monitored, so a ransomware hit during harvest does not spoil product or stop shipments.

Winery POS & Club Data

PCI-aware protection for tasting-room point-of-sale, wine-club, and e-commerce systems that hold customer and card data, with email hardened against fraud.

Immutable Backup

Backups isolated from the production network, with tested monthly restores. For a Soledad winery or grower, a ransomware hit during crush or harvest is survivable — a full data loss is not. See backup & DR.

24/7 Monitoring & Response

Tooling backed by a human who acts. Valley operations do not keep banker's hours — neither does the monitoring. When an alert fires at 2 a.m., it is contained, not sitting in a queue until morning. The speed of modern attacks demands it.
