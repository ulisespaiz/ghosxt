Community Initiative

Ghosxt Cares

Free IT foundations for Monterey County nonprofits. We donate our time and expertise to help local 501(c)(3) organizations build a modern IT foundation — at no cost for our labor.

Apply at cares@ghosxt.com
What we provide

Everything to get your
org online — done right.

Ghosxt is a Salinas-based managed IT provider. Through Ghosxt Cares, we set your organization up with the tools it needs from day one.

Microsoft 365 Tenant Setup

We walk you through Microsoft's Nonprofit program and configure your tenant, admin accounts, and security defaults.

Custom Domain + Email

Professional email on your domain (e.g., you@yourorg.org). We handle DNS records and mail flow configuration.

Basic Website

A clean single-page website deployed on your hosting — enough to establish your online presence.

DNS Configuration

Domain routing, MX records, SPF/DKIM/DMARC — configured correctly from day one.

Onboarding Session

A 1-hour walkthrough so your team knows how to use what we built.

What your org covers

Your costs, kept minimal.

Our labor is free. You only cover the third-party costs we have no control over.

Domain registration fees (~$12/year)
Microsoft 365 Nonprofit licensing (free or discounted via Microsoft)
Any web hosting costs
Ongoing maintenance and support after setup

  • Limited to 1 organization per quarter
