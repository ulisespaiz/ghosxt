Common IT challenges for Gilroy businesses

Gilroy sits at the crossroads of agriculture, food processing, and distribution — right on the 101 and at the end of the Caltrain line. That mix creates a specific set of IT problems. These are the ones we solve most often for South Valley businesses.

  • Warehouse and distribution Wi-Fi. Barcode scanners, label printers, and forklift-mounted tablets fail the moment coverage drops behind a tall rack. We survey and design warehouse-grade wireless that holds up across a full distribution floor and cold storage.
  • Food-safety and processing systems. Temperature logging, traceability, and ERP systems cannot go dark mid-shift. We keep them monitored, patched, and backed up so an audit or a recall request never catches you flat-footed.
  • Seasonal staff onboarding and offboarding. Ag and food operations scale headcount up and down with the season. We make account creation and — just as important — account removal fast and clean, so former workers never keep access.
  • Multi-shift uptime. A packing or distribution line that runs two or three shifts has no quiet window for outages. We schedule maintenance carefully and build in failover so the night shift is as covered as the day shift.
  • Retail and outlet POS. For Gilroy's outlet and retail businesses, we keep PCI-compliant point-of-sale and guest Wi-Fi reliable through weekend and holiday traffic.
