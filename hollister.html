Three economies in one Hollister

Hollister sits at the intersection of three economies that don't usually share a county: aerospace and small-aircraft tenants at Hollister Municipal Airport, working ranches and vineyards along Highway 25, and a growing manufacturing base on Industrial Drive and Fairview Road. We work across all three.

Manufacturing and machine shops typically need network segmentation between business and shop-floor systems, plus stable VPN access for vendors. Wineries and tasting rooms need POS and reservation system uptime during weekends. Aerospace tenants come in with ITAR and CUI requirements that look more like a defense contractor than a small business. We have configured for each.

Hollister is also one of the licensed cannabis cultivation areas in the region — a sector with its own track-and-trace and Metrc reporting requirements. We have built that stack too.
