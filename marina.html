Common IT challenges for Marina businesses

Marina is one of the fastest-growing parts of the Monterey Bay — built on the old Fort Ord footprint, anchored by CSU Monterey Bay, and increasingly home to ag-tech and small startups. Growth is the theme, and so are its IT problems.

  • Outgrowing the original setup. The network and email setup that worked at five people buckles at twenty. We build a cloud-first foundation that scales cleanly so you are not ripping it out every time you hire.
  • New-office buildouts. Marina has a lot of new commercial space. We handle cabling specs, Wi-Fi design, internet provisioning, and security from day one of a move — not as an afterthought.
  • Compliance from bigger customers. As local startups and ag-tech firms land larger clients, those clients start asking security questions. We put the controls and documentation in place so you can answer them and win the contract.
  • Identity and access for growing teams. More hires means more accounts, devices, and ways to get breached. We standardize MFA, device management, and least-privilege access before it becomes a mess.
  • Hybrid and student-adjacent workforces. With CSUMB next door, many Marina employers run hybrid and part-time teams. We make secure remote access simple for everyone.

FAQs about IT services in Marina

Do you support growing businesses?
Yes. Marina is one of the fastest-growing parts of the Monterey Peninsula, and we work with growing companies in the area. Plans scale from very small teams on the flat-rate Tiny Team Managed Security tier up through Compliance & Continuity for businesses with regulatory or uptime exposure.
How fast can you get on-site?
Same-day or next-day for non-emergencies. Most issues are resolved remotely the same hour.
What does managed IT cost?
Pricing is published. Core Managed IT is $125 per user per month, Secure Growth is $175, and Compliance & Continuity is $250. Teams of 1 to 4 users use the $600/mo flat Tiny Team Managed Security plan. See the full pricing page for what's included.
Are you affordable for very small businesses?
Yes. Fit depends more on operating profile and compliance exposure than headcount. Very small teams (1 to 4 users) use the flat-rate Tiny Team Managed Security plan; everyone else uses per-user tiers.
Can you set up IT for a new Marina office or buildout?
Yes. We handle the full buildout — cabling specs, Wi-Fi design, internet provisioning, and security baselines — so a new space is ready and secure from the first day your team moves in.
Can you help us pass a customer's security review?
Yes. We put the controls and documentation in place — MFA, backups, access policies, written procedures — so when a larger client sends a security questionnaire, you can answer it and win the work.
