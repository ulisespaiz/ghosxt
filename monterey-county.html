FAQs about IT services in Monterey County

Do you cover all of Monterey County?
Yes. We are based in Salinas and serve businesses across Monterey County on-site — Salinas, the Monterey Peninsula (Monterey, Pacific Grove, Carmel, Seaside, Marina), and South County (Soledad, Greenfield, Gonzales, King City). Most issues are resolved remotely the same day, with on-site visits scheduled across the county.
What industries do you support in Monterey County?
The county's core industries: agriculture and food processing in the Salinas Valley, hospitality and tourism on the Peninsula, defense contractors near NPS and DLI, healthcare and dental, professional services, and logistics. We tailor IT and security to each.
Are you local, or a Bay Area firm driving down?
Local. Ghosxt is based in Salinas, in the heart of Monterey County, run by a cleared DoD IT engineer. On-site response across the county is genuinely local, not a once-a-month drive from over the hill.
How much does managed IT cost in Monterey County?
Pricing is published upfront. Core Managed IT is $125 per user per month, Secure Growth is $175, and Compliance & Continuity is $250. Very small teams of 1 to 4 users use a $600/month flat Tiny Team Managed Security plan. See the full pricing page.
