Common IT challenges for Pacific Grove businesses

Pacific Grove is a town of small, careful operations — Victorian inns, downtown shops on Lighthouse Avenue, nonprofits, and one- and two-person professional offices. The IT problems here are small-business problems, and these are the ones we fix most.

  • Inn and B&B systems. A booking that does not sync or a guest Wi-Fi outage shows up in the reviews. We keep reservation systems, property-management software, and guest networks reliable through peak season.
  • Salt-air hardware failures. Coastal corrosion quietly kills cheap routers and switches. We spec gear that survives the environment and monitor it so a failure is caught before it takes the business down.
  • Tiny teams with no IT. When the “IT person” is also the owner, things slip. We act as the outsourced IT department — backups, security, and a real person to call — without the cost of a hire.
  • Aging buildings and wiring. PG's historic buildings were not wired for modern business. We design Wi-Fi and networking that work around old walls and limited cabling.
  • Data backup that actually exists. Many small offices think they have backups until they need one. We put real, tested backup and recovery in place so a lost laptop or failed drive is a non-event.

FAQs about IT services in Pacific Grove

Do you support inns and B&Bs?
Yes. We handle reservations system uptime, secure guest Wi-Fi, PCI-compliant payment processing, and the network reliability hospitality businesses need.
Are you affordable for very small businesses?
Yes. Many local clients are small inns, retreats, and professional offices. Very small teams (1 to 4 users) use the flat-rate Tiny Team Managed Security plan, and the per-user tiers start at 5 users.
How fast can you get on-site?
Same-day or next-day for non-emergencies. Most issues are resolved remotely the same hour.
What does managed IT cost?
Pricing is published. Core Managed IT is $125 per user per month, Secure Growth is $175, and Compliance & Continuity is $250. Teams of 1 to 4 users use the $600/mo flat Tiny Team Managed Security plan. See the full pricing page for what's included.
Do you support one- and two-person offices?
Absolutely — that is much of Pacific Grove. The flat-rate Tiny Team Managed Security plan is built for 1 to 4 users and gives a very small office real backups, security, and support without a per-hour surprise.
Can most support be handled remotely?
Yes. The large majority of issues are resolved remotely, usually within the hour. When something needs hands-on work, we are a short drive away for same-day or next-day on-site help.
Do you handle reservation systems and guest Wi-Fi for an inn?
Yes. We keep booking and property-management systems reliable, set up secure and separate guest Wi-Fi, and monitor everything so a busy weekend does not turn into a bad review.
