Onboarding & Engagement

No surprise fees. Here's exactly what onboarding costs and what the engagement looks like at every tier.

Foundation onboarding

First month covers it

For tiny teams (1 to 4 users), your first monthly payment of $600 covers all setup work: documenting your environment, deploying agents, configuring MFA, M365 hardening, and getting your team trained. No separate fee.

Per-user tier onboarding

$1,500 – $4,000 one-time

  • 5 to 15 users: $1,500
  • 15 to 30 users: $2,500
  • 30 to 50 users: $4,000
  • 50+ users: Custom quote

Covers environment audit, agent deployment, identity hardening, migration work where needed, security baseline setup, and team training. Charged once at the start of the engagement.

Engagement terms

  • 12-month standard agreement on all tiers
  • Month-to-month available at +15%
  • Cancel anytime with 30 days written notice
  • No price increases within your initial term
  • You own your data, exported on request
