FAQs about IT services in Salinas

Do you actually come on-site to Salinas businesses?
Yes. We're based in the Monterey County area and on-site visits to Salinas are routine. Most issues are resolved remotely the same day, and on-site work is scheduled within 24 to 48 hours for non-emergencies.
Do you work with agriculture and food processing companies?
Yes. Many of our Salinas clients are in agriculture, food processing, distribution, and supply chain. We understand the cybersecurity and uptime requirements of operations that cannot afford downtime during a harvest or shipping window. See our agriculture and agribusiness IT services for the full picture: FSMA traceability, GAP and SQF audit prep, cold-chain alerting, and produce ERP uptime.
