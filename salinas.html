Salinas Microsoft 365 Support

Almost every Salinas business we work with runs on Microsoft 365: Outlook, Teams, SharePoint, OneDrive, and the identity that sits underneath all of it in Entra ID. The platform is excellent. Most tenants are configured poorly out of habit. Legacy authentication is still on. MFA is partial. Conditional Access is empty. Admin accounts are shared. Default sharing is open. The result is a tenant that looks fine until it does not.

Our Microsoft 365 support in Salinas covers tenant setup and migration, baseline hardening (Conditional Access, Defender for Business, Intune device policy, legacy auth disabled, MFA enforced, admin accounts separated), SharePoint and Teams structure, email security (DMARC, DKIM, SPF, advanced filtering), licensing review, and ongoing administration. We will also tell you if you are paying for licenses you are not using.

The Microsoft 365 baseline hardening work is included in every Ghosxt managed IT tier. Standalone M365 projects are also available. See cloud services for the full picture, or read identity hardening for the 5-employee company for the controls that actually matter.

Salinas Backup and Disaster Recovery

Backups are the boring piece of IT that nobody thinks about until ransomware hits, a fileserver dies, or someone permanently deletes the wrong folder in SharePoint. We design and monitor backup systems for Salinas businesses so that the worst day of the year is a few hours of restore work, not a closed business.

Our backup and disaster recovery approach covers Microsoft 365 (Exchange, SharePoint, OneDrive, Teams) with a dedicated third-party backup, on-prem and virtualized servers, line-of-business databases, immutable cloud copies that cannot be encrypted by an attacker who already has admin, and documented restore testing on a real schedule. We also write a one-page recovery plan that an operations manager can actually follow if the engineer is not available.

This is especially important for Salinas agriculture, food processing, and logistics operations where a 48-hour outage during peak season is not survivable. See our backup and disaster recovery service for the engineering details.

Salinas Network Support

Network issues are usually the first symptom of a deeper problem and the last thing a business owner wants to debug at 7am. We handle Salinas network design and support across offices, packing sheds, coolers, warehouses, retail, and clinics: firewalls, switching, VLAN segmentation, site-to-site VPN, secure remote access, and wireless coverage that actually reaches the back of the warehouse without dead zones.

We work with the carriers that actually serve Salinas and Monterey County (Comcast Business, AT&T Fiber where it reaches, Hartnell-area fiber, and fixed wireless where wireline is not available) and design failover so a single circuit outage is not a business outage. Security is built into the design, not bolted on later: segmented IoT, separate guest Wi-Fi, no flat networks, and documented configurations.

See network design for the full engineering approach.
