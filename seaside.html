Seaside has a different economy

Seaside has the same proximity to Monterey as Pacific Grove or Carmel, but a very different economy: working-class small businesses on Fremont Boulevard and Broadway, a substantial veteran population tied to the former Fort Ord and current military presence, the CSUMB campus straddling the city line, and a healthcare and child-care services base that supports both communities.

We work with auto shops, restaurants, dental practices, retail storefronts, and nonprofits — the businesses that are not going to spend $5,000 a month on IT but still need real engineering, real backup, and real security. Our pricing was built for exactly this market.

For nonprofits in particular, we run programs through Ghosxt Cares that cover the basic IT foundation at no labor cost: Microsoft 365 setup, custom domain and email, and a basic site. If you are a 501(c)(3) in Seaside, talk to us before you pay a vendor.

Why Seaside businesses pick Ghosxt

Seaside has been growing for years and the IT options have not kept up. Most local shops are too small to invest in modern tools, and the Bay Area MSPs do not bother. We split the difference: government-grade engineering at small-business pricing, with on-site response that is a 10-minute drive.
