Common IT challenges for Seaside businesses

Seaside is a working town — retail and storefronts, auto shops, healthcare and dental offices, restaurants, and small businesses serving a growing, diverse community near CSU Monterey Bay. The IT problems here are practical, and these are the ones we fix most.

  • Retail and restaurant POS. A frozen card terminal during a rush costs real money. We keep PCI-compliant point-of-sale, receipt printers, and back-office systems running reliably.
  • No budget for “enterprise” IT. Local shops have been priced out of modern tools and ignored by Bay Area MSPs. We deliver the high-impact basics — MFA, backups, endpoint protection, patching — at small-business pricing.
  • Healthcare and dental compliance. Seaside practices handle patient data and need HIPAA-grade security and backup. We build the controls that keep them compliant and protected.
  • Aging or inherited equipment. Many businesses are running on whatever was set up years ago. We stabilize what works, replace what is unsafe, and document the whole environment.
  • Break-fix that never fixes. Calling someone only when things break means problems repeat. We monitor proactively so issues get caught before they stop the business.

FAQs about IT services in Seaside

Do you support small businesses in Seaside?
Yes. From a one-person consultancy to a 25-employee retailer, we work with the kind of small businesses that built Seaside.
How fast can you get on-site?
Same-day or next-day for non-emergencies. Most issues are resolved remotely the same hour.
What does managed IT cost?
Pricing is published. Core Managed IT is $125 per user per month, Secure Growth is $175, and Compliance & Continuity is $250. Teams of 1 to 4 users use the $600/mo flat Tiny Team Managed Security plan. See the full pricing page for what's included.
Are you affordable for very small businesses?
Yes. Fit depends more on operating profile and compliance exposure than headcount. Very small teams (1 to 4 users) use the flat-rate Tiny Team Managed Security plan; everyone else uses per-user tiers.
Do you support retail and restaurant point-of-sale?
Yes. We keep PCI-compliant POS, receipt printers, and back-office systems reliable, with secure, separate guest Wi-Fi where needed, so a rush never gets stopped by a frozen terminal.
Can you take over from break-fix IT?
Yes, and that is most of what we do here. We move you from calling someone only when things break to proactive monitoring that catches problems first — and we document the environment during the handoff.
Do you work with healthcare and dental offices in Seaside?
Yes. We build HIPAA-grade security, encryption, and backup for medical and dental practices so patient data stays protected and compliant.
