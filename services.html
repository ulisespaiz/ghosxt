IT Services for California Small Business

Most California small businesses do not need every IT service under the sun. They need the few that match how their business actually runs. Below is the full Ghosxt service catalog. Pick what you came for, or read about how we work and reach out — we will tell you straight which ones you need and which you do not. On-site across Monterey County, remote across the United States.

What we do

Managed IT Services

Fixed-fee, all-inclusive day-to-day IT for businesses of 1 to 50 employees. Monitoring, helpdesk, patching, identity, backup, and quarterly strategy. The relationship most small businesses should have with their IT provider, run by one cleared engineer instead of a ticket queue.

Learn more

Cybersecurity

Government-grade cybersecurity controls, sized and priced for small business. EDR, identity hardening, MFA, immutable backup, vulnerability management, security awareness training, and incident response. Built around the discipline of a DoD-cleared engineer.

Learn more

Cloud Services & Microsoft 365

Microsoft 365 done right, Azure where it earns its place, hybrid where it makes sense. Tenant hardening, Conditional Access, M365 migrations, and SharePoint architecture without the buzzwords or the lock-in.

Learn more

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Immutable backups, monthly tested restores, ransomware-ready DR, and RTO and RPO commitments tied to your actual business cost. The kind of backup program a DoD engineer would trust their own data on.

Learn more

Network Design

Properly designed networks. VLAN segmentation, real firewalls, enterprise wireless, multi-site VPN or SD-WAN, full documentation. No more flat networks defended by an ISP router with a default password.

Learn more

Web Development

Custom websites and e-commerce that load fast, rank well, and actually convert visitors into customers. Built from scratch by the engineer who will keep them running, hosted on infrastructure we control end-to-end.

Learn more

C-TPAT Compliance

C-TPAT cybersecurity for importers, carriers, freight forwarders, and 3PLs. CBP-aligned controls and documentation that hold up under validation, without disrupting your operations.

Learn more
