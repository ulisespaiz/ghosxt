What we do

Managed IT Services

Fixed-fee, all-inclusive day-to-day IT for small business teams of all sizes, from flat-rate Tiny Team Managed Security for 1 to 4 users up through per-user tiers for larger teams. Monitoring, helpdesk, patching, identity, backup, and quarterly strategy. The relationship most small businesses should have with their IT provider, run by one cleared engineer instead of a ticket queue.

IT Help Desk & Support

Live, US-based IT help desk and end-user support: fast response, remote and on-site fixes, and a real engineer who knows your environment instead of a tier-1 script. Password resets to printers to Microsoft 365, handled the same hour. Included with managed IT.

IT Consulting & vCIO
